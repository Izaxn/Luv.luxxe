Before You Arrive

Your Pre-Glam Checklist

A little prep goes a long way. Here's how to get the most out of your appointment.

💧
Stay Hydrated
Drink water the day before and morning of. Hydrated skin = flawless finish.
🧴
Cleanse Your Face
Come with a fresh, clean face — no heavy moisturizer or SPF.
🌿
Moisturise Lightly
A light fragrance-free moisturiser is fine. Let it absorb fully first.
😌
Skip the Makeup
Arrive bare-faced. That includes primer, tinted moisturiser, and concealer.
⏰
Be On Time
Late fees apply after 10 mins. After 15 mins, the appointment is cancelled.
